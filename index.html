About Us

At Ecoloop we believe that responsible waste disposal and recycling should be simple, rewarding, and impactful. Our mission is to empower individuals, businesses, and communities to take charge of their waste through a smart, technology-driven approach. With the Philippines generating over 21 million metric tons of waste annually and a recycling rate of only 28%, improper waste disposal has led to pollution, flooding, and health risks. Many people are unaware of proper recycling methods, and incentives for responsible waste management are limited. Ecoloop is here to change that.

Mission

Our mission is to create a cleaner, greener Philippines by providing an accessible platform that empowers communities to adopt sustainable waste management practices. We strive to build a waste-conscious society where recycling is a norm, waste is minimized, and environmental sustainability is prioritized.

Why This Works

Aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), Ecoloop actively supports global sustainability efforts while solving local waste management challenges.

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities & Communities

Making urban living cleaner and greener through smart waste management systems and accessible recycling infrastructure.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption & Production

Encouraging mindful waste management by providing tools, education, and incentives for sustainable choices.

SDG 13: Climate Action

Reducing pollution and carbon emissions for a better planet through increased recycling rates and waste reduction.

Starting in Metro Manila, we aim to expand across the Philippines and Southeast Asia, creating a future where waste is seen as an opportunity rather than a burden.
